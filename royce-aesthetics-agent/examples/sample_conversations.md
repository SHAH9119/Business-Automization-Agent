**Agent:** Great that you're doing your research first! 😊 Lip fillers are one of the most popular aesthetic treatments.

- Hyaluronic acid gel is injected to add volume and definition

Whether it's right for *you* specifically is something the dermatologist will assess at consultation — they'll look at your lip shape, discuss what you want, and make sure it's appropriate.
