## Doctor & Clinical Team

### Lead Dermatologist
- **Board-certified dermatologist** with 14+ years in medical and aesthetic dermatology
- Performs or directly supervises all medical and aesthetic procedures
- Specializes in: acne, pigmentation, hair loss, laser treatments, injectables, hair transplant planning

### Clinical Support Team
- **Reception & patient coordination** — appointments, follow-ups, billing
- **Clinical assistants / nurses** — treatment prep, post-care guidance, laser and peel assistance
- **Hair transplant team** — trained technicians under dermatologist supervision for FUE/DHI procedures

### Care Philosophy
> *"Every patient deserves a proper assessment before any treatment. We do not push procedures — we recommend what is medically appropriate and explain realistic expectations."*

- No treatment without consultation (except where a returning patient continues an approved course)
- No guarantees of results — ethical, evidence-based practice
- No cosmetic surgery (no rhinoplasty surgery, liposuction, or general plastic surgery)
- Focus: **non-surgical and minimally invasive** dermatology and aesthetics
