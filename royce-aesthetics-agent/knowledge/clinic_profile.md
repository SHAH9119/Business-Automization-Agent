# Clinic Profile — Royal Aesthetic Clinic (Royce Aesthetics)

## Overview

Royal Aesthetic Clinic (publicly branded as **Royce Aesthetics**) is a premier dermatology, hair restoration, and aesthetic clinic located in **Bahria Town Phase 7, Rawalpindi**. The clinic operates from a well-known commercial strip on the Expressway, Sector F, above "The Coffee Bean and Tea Leaf."

The clinic positions itself as a **patient-first, results-driven aesthetic center** led by a certified dermatologist with over **14 years of dermatology experience**. Their track record includes **16,200+ satisfied patients**, making them one of the most trusted aesthetic destinations in the twin cities of Rawalpindi and Islamabad.
