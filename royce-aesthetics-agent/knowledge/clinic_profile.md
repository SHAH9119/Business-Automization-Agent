# Clinic Profile

Royce Aesthetics is a dermatology and beauty clinic located in Bahria Town Phase 7, Rawalpindi.

The clinic describes itself as patient-centric and focused on personalized skin and hair care. Public website information says the clinic is led by a certified dermatologist and has 14+ years of dermatology experience.

The website claims 16,200+ happy patients over the years.

Public contact number: 0335-6377775.

Public appointment/contact timing: 7 days a week, 12 PM to 5 PM.

The website includes a form where visitors can share their name, phone number, and skin or hair concern to make an appointment or request a callback.
