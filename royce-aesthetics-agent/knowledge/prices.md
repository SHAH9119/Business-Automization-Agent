## HOW THE AGENT REPLIES TO PRICE QUESTIONS

**Correct format:**
> "Laser hair removal starts from around PKR 2,000–5,000 per session depending on the area. Final pricing is confirmed after consultation. Would you like me to book an appointment for you?"

**For hair transplants:**
> "Hair transplant cost depends on the number of grafts needed, which is assessed at consultation. Packages typically start from PKR 60,000. Shall I note a consultation request for you?"

**For injectables:**
> "Botox and filler prices vary based on the areas treated and the amount used. The dermatologist confirms the exact amount at your assessment. Starting estimates are around PKR 12,000–25,000 per area."

**Always end with:**
> "Would you like me to note an appointment request so the clinic team can give you an exact quote?"
