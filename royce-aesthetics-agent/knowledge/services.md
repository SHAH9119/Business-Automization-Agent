### 1.2 Aesthetic Skin Treatments

Premium, non-surgical procedures for skin rejuvenation, brightening, and anti-ageing.

#### HydraFacial
A multi-step facial treatment using a medical-grade device to cleanse, exfoliate, extract impurities, and hydrate the skin simultaneously. Suitable for most skin types — the dermatologist confirms suitability at consultation. No downtime.
- **Sessions:** 1 session / monthly maintenance recommended
- **Good for:** Dull skin, open pores, mild acne, dry or oily skin, fine lines

#### Chemical Peels
Controlled application of a chemical solution to exfoliate dead skin cells and stimulate new cell growth.
- **Types:** Superficial, medium, and deep peels (based on skin concern)
- **Good for:** Pigmentation, acne scars, dullness, skin texture, fine lines, melasma
- **Downtime:** Mild peeling for 3–5 days (depending on peel depth)

#### Microneedling (Dermaroller / Collagen Induction Therapy)
Fine needles create micro-channels in the skin, triggering natural collagen production.
- **Good for:** Acne scars, enlarged pores, skin laxity, stretch marks, overall rejuvenation
- **Sessions:** 3–6 sessions recommended, 4 weeks apart

#### Microneedling with PRP (Vampire Facial)
Combines microneedling with Platelet-Rich Plasma for enhanced skin regeneration.
- **Good for:** Acne scars, anti-ageing, skin brightening, uneven texture

#### HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) — Skin Tightening
Non-invasive skin tightening and lifting using focused ultrasound energy to stimulate collagen deep in the skin.
- **Good for:** Sagging skin, jawline, cheeks, neck, brow lift effect
- **Downtime:** None

#### Thread Lift (PDO Threads)
Minimally invasive face or neck lifting using dissolvable PDO (Polydioxanone) threads inserted under the skin.
- **Good for:** Jowls, cheeks, brow, neck sagging — natural-looking lift
- **Effect Duration:** 12–18 months

#### Laser Skin Resurfacing
Laser energy removes the outer skin layers and stimulates fresh collagen production.
- **Good for:** Fine lines, wrinkles, sun damage, acne scars, skin texture

#### Carbon Laser Peel (Hollywood Peel)
Carbon lotion is applied then activated by laser — deep pore cleansing, oil control, and brightening in one session.
- **Good for:** Oily/acne-prone skin, open pores, dull skin, blackheads

#### Glutathione Skin Brightening
IV or injectable glutathione for systemic skin brightening and antioxidant effect.
- **Good for:** Dark complexion, uneven tone, skin health from within

#### Mole Removal (No Mole)
Safe removal of unwanted moles, skin tags, and benign growths using laser or surgical excision.
- **Good for:** Cosmetic mole removal, suspicious lesion referral to pathology if needed

## 2. HAIR SERVICES

### 2.1 Hair Restoration — Surgical

#### FUE Hair Transplant (Follicular Unit Extraction)
Individual hair follicles are extracted from a donor area (back of head) and implanted into thinning or bald areas. A minimally invasive technique that leaves minimal scarring — the dermatologist assesses suitability at consultation.
- **Good for:** Male and female pattern baldness, receding hairline, crown thinning
- **Grafts:** Based on assessment (typically 1,000–5,000 grafts)

#### DHI Hair Transplant (Direct Hair Implantation)
Advanced FUE variation using a Choi Implanter Pen for precise depth, angle, and direction control.
- **Good for:** Natural-looking dense results, minimal downtime

#### Beard Transplant
Hair follicles transplanted to sparse or patchy beard areas for a fuller beard.
- **Good for:** Patchy beard, scarring, desire for denser beard

#### Eyebrow Transplant
Precision transplant of hair follicles into brow area for long-lasting eyebrow restoration. Results are assessed by the dermatologist.
- **Good for:** Over-plucked brows, alopecia, scarring, thin brows

---

### 2.2 Hair Restoration — Non-Surgical

#### Hair PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma)
Patient's own blood is processed to extract growth factor-rich plasma, then injected into the scalp to stimulate dormant follicles and reduce hair fall.
- **Sessions:** 4–6 sessions recommended, monthly
- **Good for:** Early-stage hair thinning, diffuse hair loss, post-transplant support

#### Hair Fillers
Injectable hair fillers (often containing hyaluronic acid and growth factors) improve scalp health and hair density.
- **Sessions:** 3–4 sessions
- **Good for:** Hair thinning, scalp dehydration, hair fall

#### Hair Growth Threads (Hair Threads)
Dissolvable threads placed in the scalp to improve blood circulation and stimulate hair growth.
- **Good for:** Diffuse hair loss, scalp health improvement

#### Scalp Micropigmentation (SMP)
A cosmetic tattooing technique that creates the appearance of a shaved head or fills in thin areas. Non-surgical, long-lasting cosmetic option.
- **Good for:** Complete baldness, thinning hairline, FUE scar concealment

#### Low-Level Laser Therapy (LLLT)
Laser light energy stimulates hair follicles for improved growth. Usually combined with other treatments.

### 2.3 Hair Removal

#### Laser Hair Removal
Permanent hair reduction using medical-grade laser technology targeting hair follicles.
- **Areas:** Upper lip, chin, underarms, arms, legs, bikini, full body
- **Sessions:** 6–8 sessions for optimal results

## 3. AESTHETIC INJECTABLE TREATMENTS

### Botox (Botulinum Toxin Injections)
FDA-approved neurotoxin injected into facial muscles to relax them and smooth out dynamic wrinkles.
- **Areas:** Forehead lines, frown lines (11 lines), crow's feet, brow lift, lip flip, neck bands
- **Duration:** 3–6 months
- **Good for:** Anti-ageing, wrinkle prevention, hyperhidrosis (excessive sweating)

### Dermal Fillers
Hyaluronic acid-based injectable gels that restore volume and enhance facial contours.
- **Areas:** Lips (lip filler), cheeks, nasolabial folds, jawline, chin, under-eye hollows (tear trough)
- **Duration:** 6–18 months depending on area
- **Good for:** Volume loss, lip enhancement, facial contouring

### Skin Boosters (Hydrafiller / Profhilo)
Injectable hydration treatment using ultra-pure hyaluronic acid to bioremodel the skin.
- **Good for:** Dull skin, crepey skin, skin quality improvement — face, neck, hands

### Mesotherapy
Micro-injections of vitamins, enzymes, and plant extracts into the skin for rejuvenation.
- **Good for:** Skin brightening, hydration, hair loss reduction on scalp
